Welcome to Nairobi's Finest Artisan Bakery

At SweetCrumbs Bakery, we combine traditional European baking techniques with the finest local Kenyan ingredients. From crusty golden sourdough to melt-in-your-mouth pastries, every item is crafted by hand and baked fresh every single morning. Experience true craftsmanship you can taste.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in the heart of Nairobi, SweetCrumbs Bakery started as a small kitchen passion project and has grown into a beloved community cornerstone. Our mission is simple: to bring joy to your table through uncompromised quality, authentic methods, and premium, locally-sourced ingredients. We believe that good bread takes time, and great pastries require love.
